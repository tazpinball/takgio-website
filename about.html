About TAKGIO

We're an AI product studio focused on two industries where AI can make the biggest difference — construction and legal. We build products, not prototypes.

The Studio

TAKGIO is an AI product studio. We're not a consultancy that advises on AI strategy — we build the products. We're not a dev shop that builds whatever you ask — we bring deep domain expertise in construction and legal/compliance to every engagement.

We exist because we believe the industries that stand to gain the most from AI are the ones getting the least attention. Construction and legal are two of the most document-intensive, process-heavy industries in the world — and they've been underserved by generic AI tools that don't understand their workflows.

Every product we build is purpose-fit for its domain, powered by Claude from Anthropic, and designed for production use from day one.

Our Approach

Four principles that guide how we build.

Domain-First

We start with the industry, not the technology. Understanding construction workflows or legal processes at a deep level is what separates a useful AI product from a tech demo. We invest the time to learn the domain before writing a line of code.

Production-Grade

Every product we build is designed to ship to real users in real environments. We don't do proofs-of-concept that sit on a shelf. If it's not going to production, we're not building it.

Responsible AI

Construction and legal are high-stakes, regulated industries. We chose Claude specifically because Anthropic's safety-first architecture aligns with the reliability and trust these industries require. Our products are built to be explainable and accountable.

Iterative by Design

AI products get better with use. We build feedback loops into every product from day one, and we maintain ongoing relationships with clients to continuously improve what we've deployed. Launch is the beginning, not the end.

Our Partnership with Anthropic

TAKGIO is a Claude partner, building exclusively on Anthropic's AI platform. We chose Claude for its industry-leading capabilities in long document processing, its safety-first architecture for regulated industries, and its enterprise-grade reliability. Our deep integration with Claude means our clients get the most out of what is arguably the most capable AI foundation available today.
