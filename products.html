Ann Elise

AI tutors that actually teach.

Ann Elise is our AI tutor for college Organic Chemistry, built on one principle: teach, don't answer. Like your best TA, she asks the right questions, builds on what you know — and never just hands you the answer.

  • Socratic dialogue — guides you to the answer instead of handing it over
  • Learns your course — your syllabus, your textbook, even how your professor teaches and tests
  • Renders 2D & 3D molecules inline, right in the conversation
  • Voice tutoring “On the Go” — talk through tough concepts; the text stays right there
  • Readiness rings track every exam, problem set, and paper at a glance
