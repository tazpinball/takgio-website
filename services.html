Construction

AI for Construction Teams

Construction generates massive volumes of documents, communications, and compliance data. We build AI products that help teams process it faster and miss less.

Document Intelligence

Automated processing and analysis of RFIs, submittals, change orders, and project correspondence. Claude reads, classifies, and extracts key information so your team can focus on decisions, not data entry.

Safety & Compliance Automation

AI-powered review of safety documentation, incident reports, and regulatory filings. Automatically flag gaps, track compliance status, and generate required documentation.

Project Communication Analysis

Intelligent monitoring of project emails, meeting notes, and daily logs. Surface action items, track commitments, and identify risks buried in the communication stream.

Estimating & Bid Support

AI-assisted analysis of bid documents, scope comparison, and historical cost data. Reduce estimating time and improve accuracy by leveraging Claude's ability to process long, complex documents.

Legal & Compliance

AI for Legal Teams

Legal work is built on language — contracts, regulations, filings, correspondence. Claude's deep language understanding makes it a natural fit for legal AI products.

Contract Analysis & Review

Automated contract review that identifies risk clauses, missing provisions, non-standard terms, and obligation conflicts. Turn hours of manual review into minutes of focused decision-making.

Regulatory Change Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of regulatory updates across jurisdictions. Automatically flag changes relevant to your practice areas and clients, with plain-language impact summaries.

Compliance Documentation

Intelligent generation and review of compliance documents, policies, and audit trails. Ensure consistency, completeness, and alignment with current regulatory requirements.

Due Diligence Automation

Accelerate M&A and transaction due diligence with AI-powered document review. Extract key terms, flag risks, and generate structured summaries across large document sets.
